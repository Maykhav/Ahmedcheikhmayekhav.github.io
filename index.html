03 ⁄ Projects

          
Systems I’ve shipped

          
Not toy projects. Engineering problems solved end-to-end.

        


        
        

          

            

              01
              IoT · RF · Control Systems
              Flagship
            

            
Smart Street Lighting Network

            

              Problem: Traditional street lighting wastes up to 40% of energy running at full
              power regardless of ambient conditions, pedestrian presence, or time of night.
            

            

              Solution: A distributed IoT mesh using XBee RF modules (868 MHz) connecting
              luminaire-level nodes — each with ambient light sensing, motion detection, and STM32-controlled
              PWM dimming. A central coordinator aggregates all data over MQTT and applies adaptive schedules
              through a real-time dashboard.
            

            
    
              
  • Adaptive dimming based on live LDR + PIR sensor fusion
    • 
              
  • RF mesh with sub-100ms inter-node latency
    • 
              
  • Live dashboard: node status, energy counters, fault alerts
    • 
              
  • SQLite log with CSV export and threshold-based alerting
    • 
            

            

              STM32XBee 868MHzMQTTPythonPlotly DashSQLite
            

          

          

            

              
            

          

        


        
        

          

            

              02
              Embedded · Bus Protocol · Power Electronics
            

            
DALI LED Control System

            

              Problem: Industrial lighting requires standardized, bidirectional digital
              control over individually addressable fixtures — without per-fixture wiring complexity.
            

            

              Solution: An ESP32-based DALI master with a custom optocoupler-isolated bus
              interface. Full IEC 62386 protocol stack implementation: individual addressing, group
              control, scene management, and fault querying across 64 DALI slaves via a WebSocket-driven UI.
            

            
    
              
  • Complete DALI protocol stack in C on ESP32
    • 
              
  • Galvanic isolation via optocoupler — clean logic/bus separation
    • 
              
  • Real-time WebSocket scene manager (dimming, scene recall)
    • 
              
  • Supports 64 individually addressable DALI ballasts
    • 
            

            

              ESP32DALI IEC 62386OptocouplerCWebSocketKiCad
